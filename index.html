Welcome to Nyawa Mixed day and Boarding

HeadTeacher's Message:

Nyawa is mixed day and boarding primary school which is situated 4km to the east of iten town, the school lies in the hanging valley overlooking kerio valley. The school is middle cost boarding having good learning facilities eg classrooms, dormitories ablution blocks, dining hall and enough learning resources. The school features well in curricula and co-curricular activities. The school posts qualified motivated staff and non-teaching staff who are responsible for high academic excellence. Learning starts from 5:00am to 9:00pm with teachers guiding learners in their respective classes.Balanced diet meals are served to the leanersProspective parents are hereby warmly welcome to bring their children to this institution.

Thank you and God bless.
